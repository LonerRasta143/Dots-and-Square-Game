Dots and Square

Welcome to Dots and Square!
The game where the goal is to claim the most squares
by completing the last connection to a square.

How to play!

  1. You start with a grid of evenly spaced dots.
  2. Players take turns drawing one straight line between two neighboring dots
    (horizontal or vertical only) by clicking between the two dots you wish to connect.
  3. When you draw the fourth side of a box, you claim that square...
    usually by writing your initial inside it.
  4. If you complete a square, you get another turn.
  5. The game ends when all possible lines are drawn.
  6. The player with the most squares wins.
